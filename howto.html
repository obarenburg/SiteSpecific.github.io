To Start

Get a group of 2-15 people and head to the destination from the locations tab. Depending on your group size pick from quarter to a half of the group to be guessers and the rest as actors

Go to the locations menu and pick the location your playing at. If you are one of the actors use the randomizer to get a task and a site specific place to do it or pass through on the way. If you are a guesser stand by and read the next section.

Now actors will go and preform their task, while the guessers watch to try and see what each persons task was. After 3 minutes go one by one through the actors and have the guessers come to a consensus of what they think the persons task was.

Think about what could throw off the guessers! If your an actor think of tasks that could be on the list based on what location your in and do those on your way to your real task. If your a guesser, try to pin point a specific action that seems very out of place. Most importantly be creative and have fun!
